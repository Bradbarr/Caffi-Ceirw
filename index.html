Caffi Ceirw
Family-run • Country Park • Warm welcome

A cosy stop in the countryside

Caffi Ceirw is a small, family-run café at Golden Grove (Gelli Aur) Country Park — a relaxed place for coffee, homemade bakes and light lunches after a walk. We also welcome community events and private functions.

About us

A family-run café with tremendous support from volunteers — rooted in the community and the outdoors.

Family-run, community-supported

Caffi Ceirw is proudly family-run, shaped by the people who visit and the park that surrounds us. We’re incredibly grateful for the tremendous support from volunteers who help keep the café welcoming, warm, and running smoothly — especially on busy days and event nights.

Whether you’re stopping in after a walk, meeting friends, or joining one of our themed evenings, we aim to offer a friendly atmosphere and simple, satisfying food.
